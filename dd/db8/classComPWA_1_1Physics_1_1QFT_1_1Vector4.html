template<typename _Tp>
class ComPWA::Physics::QFT::Vector4< _Tp >

: 4-vectors and 4-momenta.

Author
Mike Williams

This class provides extra functionality needed for 4-vectors. The class inherits from Tensor and in most cases is used as such.
